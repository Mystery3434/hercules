We are pleased to inform you that your application to become a tutor at Hercules was successful! You can now start tutoring on the website.

Firstly, you will need to fill in a short bio to tell students more about you and your tutoring style. You can do this on the "Account" page.

After this, you can enter your Skype ID and Hangouts ID on the "Account" page. Note that these will be publicly visible for students. Lessons with students will be conducted over video chat on the platform of your choice.

Next, you'll need to update the time slots when you are available for lessons. After this, you will be notified when a student books a lesson with you. You will be able to contact them on the "Messages" page.

The payment for your lessons will be done on a weekly basis. The payment structure is as follows:

1. For the first 10 lessons, you get paid $40 per lesson.
2. For the next 20 lessons, you get paid $50 per lesson.
3. For the next 30 lessons, you get paid $60 per lesson.
4. After this, you will get paid $70 per lesson.

Having more lessons not only pays you more, but also boosts your tutor ranking, leading more students towards your profile. 

Please enter your Paypal email on the "Account" page in order to ensure that the payment reaches you. This information is private and will not be visible to other users.

Happy tutoring!
